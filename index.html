Admissions

Join Our Community

We're thrilled you're considering Prime Time Academy for your child's education.

Our admissions process is designed to help us get to know your family and ensure we're the right fit for your child's educational needs.

Begin by submitting an inquiry form or attending one of our open house events. We encourage all families to schedule a campus tour to experience our community firsthand.

Complete our online application form and submit the required documents, including academic records and teacher recommendations. Our application fee is #35,000.

Applicants spend a day visiting the school to participate in classes and meet potential classmates. This helps us assess fit and ensure proper placement.

Admissions decisions are typically communicated within two weeks of completing all application requirements. Accepted students will receive enrollment contracts and information about next steps.
